How to read this
🏃 Every project races its plan on the same calendar: a runner LEFT of the white TODAY line is behind schedule; at or past it is on track — the runner and the pill always agree. 🏁 marks the last planned task of the month, │ lines are the Sunday weekly deadlines.
Every % counts worked days, not tasks: a finished task earns all of its planned days, one in progress earns the days worked so far, one not started earns 0. Example: 5 drywall days planned, 3 worked = 60%. Task counts ("8 tasks · 3 done") are context only. Admin tasks (meetings, checklists, walkthroughs) don't count — construction work only.
Pill colors: green = on pace · blinking orange = behind, but the week can still be saved if the rescheduled tasks finish by Sunday · yellow = slightly behind · blinking red = behind plan.
